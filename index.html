Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

AI WOD Timer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information — if you choose to sign in, we collect your email address via Supabase Auth. Sign-in is optional; the app works without an account.
  • Workout data — workouts you create or parse are stored locally on your device. If you are signed in, they are synced to our database so you can access them across devices.
  • Workout text for AI parsing — when you submit a workout description to be parsed, the text is sent to our backend and processed by OpenAI's API. We do not store this text beyond the duration of the request.
  • Crash and error reports — we use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports that help us fix bugs. These reports do not include personally identifiable information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect health or fitness data from HealthKit.
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and cloud sync. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI — AI workout parsing. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — crash reporting. Privacy Policy

Data Retention

If you delete your account, all associated workout data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Local data on your device is removed when you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at our GitHub repository.
